How the scoring works

Same five options for every question:

Yes= 1 point. The study clearly meets the criterion.
Partly= 0.5. Meets it incompletely or with caveats.
No= 0. Does not meet the criterion.
Cannot tell= 0. Reporting is unclear; document why.
N/Aexcluded from the denominator (use sparingly).

    How the overall judgement is computed
    • Low concern — score ≥ 75% and no more than 1 No across applicable items.
    • Some concern — score 50–74%, or 2–3 No ratings.
    • High concern — score < 50%, or 4+ No ratings.
    • Unclear — set manually if too much info is missing to judge fairly.

    Tip: add a one-line justification for any No or Cannot tell. The field appears automatically.

    About this tool

    This form supports the Risk-of-Bias / Study-Quality Appraisal section of the systematic literature review Optimizing Drone-Based Search and Rescue With ADS-B-Enabled Deconfliction in Mixed Manned-Unmanned Operations. The eight-question instrument is a modified, MMAT-informed engineering quality checklist (not the unmodified MMAT 2018).

    How to use it

    1. Open a study card from the dashboard.
    2. Click Open PDF in new tab and rate each of the eight questions.
    3. Add a short justification for any No or Cannot tell.
    4. Move to the next study or return to the dashboard — everything saves automatically.
    5. When you're done, click Download my Excel on the dashboard.

    Where your data lives

    All of your responses are stored only in this browser, on this device (localStorage). Nothing is sent anywhere. If you clear your browser data, your ratings are deleted — so export your Excel before clearing storage. Export early and often.
